# Operation Total Democracy

**Operation Total Democracy** is the **maximum-priority** mode: all squads align on one objective until the crisis is contained or the human stands down.

---

## When to activate

- Human says **`TOTAL DEMOCRACY`**, or  
- `ESCALATE` with **CRITICAL** severity and human approval, or  
- Production / security / data-loss scenario per `escalation.md`.

---

## Rules of engagement

1. **Single objective** — human states the north star in one paragraph. Everything else is `[PENDING SCOPE]`.
2. **THE AUTHENTIC** owns the live brief: stack, constraints, “do not touch” list.
3. **THE SCOUT** (or fastest read-only pass) confirms current repo state before large edits.
4. **THE FORGE** and **THE EXECUTOR** stay **separated** for any large change set (Squad B rules).
5. **THE AUDITOR** runs after each logical chunk: tests, diff, scope.
6. **THE SCRIBE** updates `PROJECT_LOG.md` after each major phase so parallel humans stay aligned.

---

## Communication cadence

Prefer short `SITREP` every logical milestone, not wall-of-text dumps.

```text
[THE AUTHENTIC] → [ALL] | SITREP | Objective: … | Phase: 1/3 | Blockers: none
```

---

## Standing down

Human says **`abort`** or **`stand down`** (define in your `AGENTS.md` if you want a softer keyword than `abort`).

- Capture final state in `PROJECT_LOG.md`.
- List **follow-up** missions (usually C or D).
